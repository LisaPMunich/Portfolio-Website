Why a career change at 37? Why become a web developer?

I loved working in project management. But ever since having two wonderful kids I noticed that finding a suitable job with a good work-life-balance was very difficult in the field of project management and marketing.
I did dabble in recruiting, but that was not for me. After doing some online courses on freecodecamp, udemy, coursera I discovered my passion for web development, programming and problem-solving. And I decided to do learn something new. And the journey so far has been amazing.
I love doing the meticulous work, to review, improve on design and details, make code cleaner. I’m always excited to learn new programming languages, expanding my understanding of the whole programming eco-system and keep up with the always evolving world of programming.
