About Me.

I am Lisa Pape and I always liked challenges.

So at 16 - I am 37 now - I travelled to Taiwan, stayed there for one year, learned Chinese.
I loved the culture and the language. So my dad encouraged me to find a job I could put that to use in. So I studied business administration, intercultural communication and business Chinese. Again I went to Asia, mainland China this time and I stayed there for 1,5 years.

With my diploma I worked in small agencies and mid-sized companies doing:

  • project management
  • market research
  • marketing

Later I worked in big corporations like BMW in the following functions:

  • branding
  • naming
  • copy writing and texting

Ever since having two wonderful kids I noticed that finding a suitable job with a good work-life-balance was very difficult in the field of project management and marketing.
I did dabble in recruiting, because that is something you can very well do part-time. Finally I discovered my passion for web development, programming and problem solving.
Hopefully I can add a lot of skills to this page very soon.

This is what I enjoy doing - apart from coding:

  • long walks with my dog
  • family time
  • Upcycling
  • Yoga
  • Indoor Climbing
