What do I bring to the table as a developer?

I’m a web developer with professional experience in product management and interface positions. I know how to organise myself within the time constraints of a project and align interests with colleagues from different departments. My experience as a branding and naming specialist has also taught me the importance of incorporating the customer perspective into the product.

Having spent two years alone in China - the first time at 16 - has taught me to self-reliantly find solutions and simply continue trying when encountering an obstacle. I did not give up easily, when I subsequently studied business Chinese, in contrary it definitely coined my drive and attention to detail.

Now as a developer, when doing a project, I do not shy away when an application suddenly throws errors, but always strive to find solutions - with the help of documentation, forums, tutorials, by rolling back or simply asking around fellow developers. Also, my attention to detail comes in handy when finding bugs and doing quality assurance.
